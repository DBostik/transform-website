## NOTES:

- Use a small cookie scooper (size


#60) to create consistently sized meatballs.

- Serve over a bed of cauliflower rice for a balanced meal. Costco even has pre-made bags of it in their frozen section. Talk about a time saver!

- Feel free to swap the ground chicken for ground turkey.
